## Description (the body)

Your best thinking now happens inside AI chat windows, and then it scrolls away. Stash keeps all of it. Every conversation, and every page you read, saved to one private memory that lives only on your machine.

Stash itself is free. **Pro** adds a small AI model, bundled right into the extension, that makes your memory work for you. It runs entirely on your device and sends nothing anywhere.

### What Pro gives you

**Ask across everything.** Ask a question and get an answer assembled from the best passages across all your saved chats and pages, with every line citing the memory it came from. Nothing is invented.

**Memory inside your chats.** A button on every AI chat surfaces your related past conversations and drops the right one into your prompt as context, without you ever leaving the page or saving by hand.

**It organizes itself.** Your memory sorts into named collections by topic, so you can finally see what you keep coming back to.

**Summaries and tags, written for you.** Summarize any thread down to a sentence or two, and let your archive tag itself.

**A memory profile.** A quiet home screen that shows what you keep, a full year of your saving habits, and a way straight back into anything.

### How it works

1. Install the free Stash extension.
2. Buy Pro here. Your license key arrives by email.
3. Open the Stash dashboard, click Activate Pro, and paste your key.

That is the whole setup. Pro keeps working offline from then on.

### Privacy is the whole point

A memory cannot leak if it never leaves. Stash makes no network calls of its own. The single time it touches the internet is the moment you activate your license, and it only asks for that permission then. No account, no cloud, no tracking, and nothing for us to sell, because we never receive it.
