Support

Stash keeps every AI conversation and the pages you read in one private memory on your device. Here is how to get going, and how to reach us.

Getting started

  1. Install the Stash extension.
  2. Open any AI chat or web page and click the Stash pill, or the toolbar icon, to save it.
  3. Open the dashboard (the stash. icon) to search, revisit, and organize everything you have kept.

Activating Stash Pro

  1. Buy Pro, and you will receive a license key by email.
  2. Open the Stash dashboard and, in the bottom left, click Activate Pro.
  3. Paste your key and confirm. Pro unlocks instantly and keeps working offline.

When you activate, your browser will ask permission for Stash to verify the key with the payment provider. That is the one and only network request Stash makes, and it is asked for only at that moment.

Common questions

My license key was not recognised.

Copy it exactly from your receipt, with no extra spaces. If it still will not take, reply to your receipt email and we will sort it fast.

Where is my data stored?

Only on your device, in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. See our privacy policy.

How do I back up or move my archive?

Open the dashboard and use Export All to save your whole archive to a file. Import it on any device to restore it.

Which sites does the pill work on?

The major AI chat tools, plus any web page you can read in your browser.
